Target Flash 11 and AIR 3 - Merging AIR SDK into Flex SDK

There are many people out there that seem to go through the laborious process of manually merging the Adobe Air SDk and Adobe’s Flex SDK every time a new iteration of either comes out.

I’ve used this method in the past, and not only is it time consuming, it’s prone to errors, having not put the right thing in the right place.

On MacOS, dumping a folder in place of another, overwrites that folder in it’s entirety. Killing any folders that don’t exist in the folder you’re copying over the top. Not ideal. Windows is the opposite – it overwrites individual files at your prompt.
